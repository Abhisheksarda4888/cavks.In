Our Story

12 Years of Excellence

VK&S is a multi-disciplinary chartered accountancy firm based in Nagpur. Founded in 2014 by three partners — CA Varun Vijaywargi, CA Pavan Khabiya and CA Ashish Saoji — the Firm has made a notable mark in a short span of time.

Over 12 years, we have grown to provide single-window professional services in Nagpur, Mumbai, Delhi, Pune, Hyderabad, Indore and Raipur — serving organisations across 12+ industries.

Our approach: combine deep domain expertise with the specific business goals of each client. Given India's ever-changing regulatory landscape, we stay proactive — not reactive.
