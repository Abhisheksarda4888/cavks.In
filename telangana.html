Overview

VK&S assists industries in Telangana in availing incentives under the state's industrial policy — from eligibility assessment and TS-iPASS clearances through claim preparation and ongoing compliance.

TS-iPASS Clearances

Fast-track clearances under Telangana State Industrial Project Approval & Self Certification System.

Package Incentive Schemes

Advisory on capital subsidy, power tariff concessions and other state incentives.

Claim Preparation

Preparation and submission of incentive claim applications with state government.

Compliance Management

Post-disbursement compliance reports and annual returns to state authorities.

Land Allotment Assistance

Coordination for TSIIC land allotment and government approvals.

IT & ITeS Incentives

Specialised advisory for IT and ITeS companies seeking Telangana tech incentives.
